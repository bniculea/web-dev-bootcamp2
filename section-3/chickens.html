Chicken

The chicken (Gallus gallus domesticus) is a domesticated red junglefowl species that is originally from India. They have also partially hybridized with other wild species of junglefowl (the grey junglefowl, Ceylon junglefowl, and green junglefowl).[1] Rooster and cock are terms for adult male birds, and a younger male may be called a cockerel.
